# Battle RPG — Controls & Gameplay Guide

## Gameplay Overview

Battle RPG is a side-view arena combat game. Select a class, start the game, and survive endless waves of enemies. Kill 5 enemies to advance to the next wave. Every 5 waves, a boss spawns. The environment changes every 5 waves (Forest, Castle, Volcano, Beach, Tundra, Swamp).

---

## Controls

### Desktop

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| **Move** | Left-click on the ground |
| **Attack (S1)** | Left-click on an enemy |
| **Special (S2)** | Right-click anywhere |
| **Special (S2)** | Long-press on mobile (0.4s hold) |

### Mobile / Touch

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| **Move** | Tap on the ground |
| **Attack (S1)** | Tap on an enemy |
| **Special (S2)** | Long-press on the screen (0.4s hold) |

## Classes

### Warrior ⚔️
- **HP:** 120 | **ATK:** 22 | **SPD:** 8
- **S1 — Shield Bash (left-click):** Strikes forward in a wide arc. Deals 100% ATK damage with knockback that pushes enemies away. Good for crowd control.
- **S2 — Sword Slash (right-click / long-press):** Fires a shockwave projectile that deals 250% ATK damage with knockback. 1-second cooldown. High damage at range.

### Mage 🔮
- **HP:** 80 | **ATK:** 18 | **SPD:** 14
- **S1 — Magic Bolt (left-click):** Fires a fast magic bolt projectile that deals 90% ATK damage on hit. Reliable single-target damage.
- **S2 — Fireball (right-click / long-press):** Launches a fireball that explodes on impact, dealing 220% ATK damage in an area-of-effect. 1-second cooldown.

### Archer 🏹
- **HP:** 70 | **ATK:** 24 | **SPD:** 18
- **S1 — Quick Shot (left-click):** Fires a fast arrow dealing 95% ATK damage. Highest base damage of S1 skills. 15% critical hit chance.
- **S2 — Arrow Barrage (right-click / long-press):** Fires 3 arrows in a spread pattern, each dealing 130% ATK damage. 1-second cooldown. Great against groups.

### Magic Gladiator ✨
- **HP:** 140 | **ATK:** 26 | **SPD:** 6
- **S1 — Dual Slash (left-click):** Swings both weapons in a wide arc dealing 110% ATK damage with a 12% crit chance. Covers a large area.
- **S2 — Cross Slash (right-click / long-press):** Creates a massive cross-shaped AoE explosion at the target location, dealing 300% ATK damage. 1-second cooldown. Highest damage in the game.

## Combat Mechanics

- **Damage:** Each attack deals damage based on your ATK stat with some randomness (±10%).
- **Critical Hits:** Some attacks have a chance to deal 200% damage (shown with 💥).
- **Knockback:** Warrior's attacks push enemies away. Warrior S2 shockwave has strong knockback.
- **Wave System:** Kill 5 enemies to advance to the next wave. Enemy strength scales with wave number (+15% HP and ATK per wave).
- **Bosses:** Every 5 waves, a boss spawns with significantly higher HP and ATK.
- **Environment:** Changes every 5 waves, cycling through 6 biomes with unique visual themes.
- **S2 Cooldown:** Special abilities have a 1-second cooldown shown as a circular timer at the bottom-right of the screen.
